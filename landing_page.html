Twas a Girl who lived her life

My name is De'Aisha Lougin. I am a Sophomore at Mississippi State University who once was a psychology major, but now has trespassed into the amazing world of being a Computer Science major. I was inspired to make this project based on a social media post on the app Tiktok, that presented a submission form for people to share their similar experiences in life. At the moment, I am currently focusing it on being a place where i can upload about my life and all the things I struggle with, but also succeed at at the same time. Given more time and experience, I hope to make this into a platform other women and people can do the same.

The reason I chose Computer Science all started with one summer. The last summer was one of the most conflicting summers I have ever had academic wise. I started off with Psychology, but knew even during the previous school year that Psychology and my original plan of becoming a Therapist was not the life/career route for me. I did not believe it was a life style cut out for me, not that i did not want to help people, but more so that i wanted to be helpful in a more interesting and interactive/technological way. I started with UX Research later also realizing research was not my perogative. Then came Web Development.
